About

Anon Ray

Yes, that’s my name. No I’m not kidding. Yeah my name means Anonymous in English, but originally it is a Bengali/Sanskrit word.

I’m a software engineer by choice, and a hacker and explorer by nature. After graduating in Computer Science & Engineering, I have mostly worked in web development. I have also hacked on wireless mesh networks a little, and in developing software infrastructure for hosting large number of applications.

My interests are - in theoretical aspects of computer science (and generally in any theoretical or abstract concept), and recently in functional programming and programming lanugage theory.

I am interested in music, in the nature, in sustainable living. I am also a birder and trek occasionally. I like intellectual ramblings over a tea, ciggarette, or better even - a joint.

Currently, I work at Hasura at Bangalore to build a powerful, yet flexible and completely custom owned BaaS + PaaS.

In the past I have worked with HashCube a gaming company, Janastu an NGO that provides open-source IT solutions to other NGOs, and in a Govt. of India project called Virtual Labs.
