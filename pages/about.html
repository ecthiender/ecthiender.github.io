About

Anon Ray

Yes, that’s my name! Yes, my name means Anonymous in English, but its intended meaning is a Bengali word.

I’m a software engineer by profession, and a hacker and explorer by nature. After graduating in CS, I have mostly worked in web applications, backend systems, and server infrastructures. I have also hacked on wireless mesh networks a bit, and software infrastructure for hosting large number of internet applications.

My broad interests are in and systems theory, programming languages and algorithms.

Apart from CS, I am intersted in music, natural history, and in sustainable living. I am also a birder and trek occasionally. I like intellectual ramblings on a lazy afternoon or past midnight (you get the point).

Currently, I work at Hasura on various products. I work on a PaaS, microservices, Kubernetes, GraphQL, and write code mostly in Haskell with smatterings of Python, Javascript and Bash.

In the past I have worked with HashCube a gaming company, Janastu an NGO that provides FOSS solutions to other NGOs/NPOs, and on a Govt. of India project called Virtual Labs.

I’m on Twitter. You can find all my code and projects on GitHub. I put up my birding photographs on Flickr. I hang out on Freenode as ecthiender.
